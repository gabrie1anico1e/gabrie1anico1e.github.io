H2S Alive

H2S is a naturally occurring gas. It is often referred to as sour gas, acid gas, stink damp or sulphurated hydrogen. H2S is colourless, heavier than air in its pure state, and extremely toxic. It can also deaden your sense of smell. In low concentrations, it has a rotten egg smell and causes eye and throat irritation. At higher concentrations, it can cause death..

Asbestos Training

This is a Government of Alberta approved 2-day classroom-based training for the Occupational Health and Safety for the Asbestos Worker Course. The course combines practical and theory designed for you to learn the skills required to work safely around asbestos and the proper protocols and asbestos abatements methods and procedures. Completion of this course provides you with the necessary certification to access a restricted area due to an asbestos hazard.

Fall Protection

Falls from height are among the most common causes of serious work-related injuries and deaths in the workplace. This comprehensive fall protection training was developed to meet the highest standards for training in most Canadian jurisdictions using Canadian Standards Associations (CSA) Z259.

Confined Space Entry and Monitor

Every year workers sustain fatal injuries in confined space. Selecting the right training courses for your health and safety needs is paramount. Our Confined Space Entry and Monitor Training is the most commonly required course. Confined Space Entry Training for Rescue is necessary only if you are going to be part of a rescue team or your employer requires you to have the rescue component.

Standard First Aid

Standard First Aid is the most commonly required training course in the workplace. In addition to CPR, this course offers valuable information and skills to a first aider so that you will be effective until EMS arrives.
