Who are We?

About

We are a family owned company established in 2009. With experience dating from 1989 taking pride in our safety procedures and diligence we put in our work.We have been removing asbestos contains construction and insulating material in every location in Alberta and Saskatchewan specializing in Industrial gas plants and commercial sites.

FOUNDER


Elmer Murga has been working in abatement since 1989, and has nearly 70,000 logged hours working with asbestos across Alberta and Saskatchewan. His specialties are in abatement, infectious control, emergency response, and remediation, with additional training in Hazardous Materials (Levels 1-3 in AB, as well as a NFPA 472 emergency hazmat technician designation). He has supervised or managed numerous large-scale projects over his career.

PREVIOUS CLIENTS

For the last 10 years we have been serving multiple Albertan clients including Keyera Canada, Energy Transfer (formerly SemCams), Husky Rainbow Lake, Embridge, and some Kinder Hordan projects. We have also worked with residential clients, doing basement renovations and sample intakes. We are here for all your asbestos needs. Doing it right all the time.
